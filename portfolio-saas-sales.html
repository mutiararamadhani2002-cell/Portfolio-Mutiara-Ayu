SaaS Sales Discount Impact Analysis

Conducted an exploratory data analysis project to analyze the impact of discount strategies on SaaS sales profitability, customer purchasing behavior, and overall business performance.

Python Pandas NumPy Matplotlib Seaborn Tableau Business Intelligence

Key Features

  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
  • Discount Strategy Analysis
  • Profitability Analysis
  • Correlation Analysis
  • Customer Segment Analysis
  • Business Dashboard Visualization

Project Overview

This project focuses on understanding how discount strategies influence SaaS sales performance, customer purchasing patterns, and profitability through data-driven analysis and visualization.

Various analytical techniques were applied to identify relationships between discount rates, sales volume, profit margins, and customer behavior in order to support business decision-making and strategy evaluation.
